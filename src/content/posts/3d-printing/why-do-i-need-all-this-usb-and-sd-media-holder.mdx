<section>
  <excerpt>
  <p>This may belong in a "yet another" category for 3D modeling, but yes I wanted to design something for managing the countless portable media and dongles I hang onto. I also wanted a rectagular plot instead of a long strip that I see in many other models. Though this isn't a remix, this was definitely inspired by a <a href="https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:2637487">USB SD and MicroSD holder</a> I've used until now.</p>
  </excerpt>

  <strong>This model can hold:</strong>
  <ul class="square">
    <li>24 USB-A sticks and/or dongles</li>
    <li>16 SD cards</li>
    <li>29 MicroSD cards</li>
  </ul>
  <hr/>
</section>
